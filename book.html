COVID-19: How to book a pool or gym time slot?

 

Please find below a step by step guide on how to book a gym time slot at our ActiveSG Gym at Fernvale

Step 1: Log in to ActiveSG App, tap on Facilities.

 

 

Step 2: Tap on Search Box, select the ActiveSG facility you plan to visit

 

 

Step 3: Select Date of Visit and time slot. (You may only book one slot per day)

 

Step 4: Click Add to Cart.

 

Step 5: Select Pay Now to go to Shopping Cart. Tap on Proceed to Pay to complete the payment.

How to enter the gym?

  1. Go to gym on the stipulated timeslot. Select MyCash to go to Active Wallet.

 

  1. Tap on Transaction History to display the receipt. Please show the receipt to our Guest Officer to be admitted. (Alternatively, you may also show the PDF receipt that is sent to your email.)
